We are here to add more color to your life
We often get that question. Yes, why rubber ducks? We actually asked ourselves that too. But we just had to do it. In 2011 I had a shop with all kinds of trendy gifts for children. My daughter came up with the idea of including some rubber ducks in the range because she liked them so much. It started with a corner and quickly grew into a larger collection. Because our customers responded so enthusiastically, we came up with the idea to overturn the entire store and turn it into a rubber duck store. But wouldn't that be a bit too drastic?
